Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What is Crackle Journal (타닥타닥)?

It's a journaling app where you enter short daily notes ("kindling"), and AI weaves them into a novel-style diary.

Q. How do I sign in?

You can sign in with Google or Apple. You can also try the app without signing in using "Browse" mode.

Q. Where are my diaries stored?

Most records are stored on your device, while account information and some feature data are stored securely in the cloud. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Q. I want to delete my account.

Select "Delete Account" from the account menu in the app to permanently delete your account and related data.

Q. My diary isn't being generated.

Please check your network connection. If the problem persists, contact us at the email above.
